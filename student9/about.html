About Kailash Satyarthi

Kailash Satyarthi won the nobel prize for fighting against the suppression of children and young people and for the right of all children to education."Every chlid should be free to be a child."-Kailash Satyarthi

Some facts about Kailash Satyarthi

  • he founded the organization Bachpan Bachao Andolan(save childhood movement), which has freed thousands of children from slave-like conditions.
  • born on January 11, 1954
  • he followed the tradition of Mahatma Gandhi
  • created a documentary called The Price of Free about child labor efforts in India
