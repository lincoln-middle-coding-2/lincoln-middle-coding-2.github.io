About Kailash Satyarthi

Kailash Satyarthi was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2014 for "fighting against the suppression of children and young people and for the right of all children to education." "Every chlid should be free to be a child."-Kailash Satyarthi

Some facts about Kailash Satyarthi

  • born on January 11, 1954
  • he followed the tradition of Mahatma Gandhi
  • he founded the organization Bachpan Bachao Andolan (save childhood movement), which has freed over 80 thousand children from slave-like conditions.
  • created a documentary called The Price of Free about child labor efforts in India
